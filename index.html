What areas do you serve?

We serve homes and offices throughout Nigeria. Contact us to confirm service availability in your area.

Yes, our team arrives fully equipped with professional cleaning products and equipment.

You can book by filling out our contact form, calling us, or sending a WhatsApp message using the QR code or floating button.

Absolutely. All our staff are vetted and trained to ensure your safety and satisfaction.

Yes, we offer eco-friendly cleaning options. Please mention your preference when booking.

We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance to secure your preferred date and time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers and select digital payment options. Details will be provided upon booking.
